A .KOD műhelye

Soma vagyok, web-designer. Jelenleg az Innonic-nál készítek felhasználóbarát weboldalakat kis- és középvállalatoknak.

A .KOD egy pár főből álló stúdió Debrecenben. Our passion is to combine design and tech skills to create tools that empower businesses and tear down problems.

We're very passionate about combining the technologies we love (like Ruby on Rails) with agile fundamentals to get things done. Our apps have been showcased in several publications around the world, some of them are showcased in the next section.

The studio was founded by Pedro Sousa and Rui Leitão who believe in treating design as an essential component of any work done. Take a moment to learn about some of our apps in the section below.
